Less Grind, More Roleplay

Tired of endlessly recycled methods of forced and filler grind that detract from the entire purpose of roleplay and character development? So are we... Please, never again.

Innovation

We've never been satisfied with the status quo. That's why we're constantly seeking innovative methods for greater levels of immersion in the game world. We're excited to bring something new to the table, finally!

Iteration

Not all older methodologies are bad. Not all innovative methods play out the same way in practice as on paper. That's why it's important to constantly push for improvement, even when you think there's none left to be achieved. We're here to do just that.

Community

At the end of the day we're all part of a community with similar goals. It's paramount to recognize this and enable positive, constructive, community interaction - minus all the bullshit. We're here to be your shield against those who would corrupt and diminish your experience.

Roleplay

We strive for quality in all aspects of our community but that of our expectations for quality roleplay are among the pinnacle. Consider us serious about quality RP. But unlike some other communities, we'll let our actions to come speak volumes.
